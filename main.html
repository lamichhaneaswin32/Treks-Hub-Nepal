Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the trek packages?
All our packages include accommodation, meals during the trek, professional guide, permits, and safety equipment. International flights and personal expenses are not included.
Do I need travel insurance?
Yes, comprehensive travel insurance covering high-altitude trekking and emergency evacuation is mandatory for all our treks.
What's the best time to trek?
The best seasons are autumn (Sep–Nov) and spring (Mar–May) when weather is clear and stable. Each season offers unique experiences.
How fit do I need to be?
Fitness requirements vary by trek. We recommend regular cardio exercise and hiking practice. We'll provide detailed preparation guidelines upon booking.
