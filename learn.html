Everest Base Camp Trek

Overview

Join us for a 14-day trek to the base of the world’s highest peak. Experience Sherpa culture, panoramic Himalayan views, and high-altitude adventure.

  • Duration: 14 Days
  • Difficulty: Challenging
  • Max Altitude: 5,364m
  • Group Size: 2–12 trekkers
  • Cost: $1,299

Route Map

Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Fly to Lukla, trek to Phakding
  2. Day 2: Trek to Namche Bazaar
  3. Day 3: Acclimatization in Namche
  4. Day 4–6: Trek through Tengboche to Dingboche
  5. Day 7–9: Reach Lobuche, Everest Base Camp
  6. Day 10–13: Descend to Lukla
  7. Day 14: Fly back to Kathmandu
